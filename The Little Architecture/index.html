The Little Architecture Company

The Little Architecture Company is based in Solihull and is led by Nicola Hopwood, a chartered architect registered with the RIBA and ARB.

We specialise in housing, delivering new-build and refurbishment projects across the West Midlands, Warwickshire and Worcestershire, and further afield in Dorset, Somerset and Shropshire.

Other sector experience includes education, leisure and retail, education and the Home Office (police/custodial).

We are currently also working on Lokrum Fields for Wider Ambition Ltd, which is an independent specialist school for able pupils with autism, anxiety and related needs.

WE OFFER:
  • CONCEPT DESIGN OPTIONS AND FEASIBILITY STUDIES
  • SECURING PLANNING PERMISSIONS
  • BUILDING REGULATIONS AND CONSTRUCTION DRAWINGS
  • CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND PROJECT MANAGEMENT ON SITE
